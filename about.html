Meet Brigid Massey

Creative Director

Superior marketing and design begins with understanding the customer and the competition. This technique is the foundation for my design process. Using the sales and marketing experience I gained while working for major Fortune 500 companies, brings the perfect blend of experience and professionalism to the planning and execution on every project that I work on. We are all connected no matter what language we speak or where we are from. Our connection to others can be unified through line and color in effective designs.

In My Free Time...

When I am not at work, I enjoy the outdoors. I live in the nations "Oldest City", St. Augustine Florida. Living near the ocean allows me to take walks on the beach and enjoy the water. I also enjoy travelling and discovering hidden gems located within my own state of Florida and within the United States. There are so many natural beauties located within driving distance and with each visit I discover something new.
